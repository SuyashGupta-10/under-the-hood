Conclusion & Key Takeaways

A 3-day AI investigation that turned data into a clear path back to profitability.

🔍

The Star-Gap is Real

31% of 4-5★ reviews carry negative sentiment. Relying on average star ratings alone is dangerously misleading for product decision-making.

🔋

Battery is the Root Cause

With 38% mention frequency and anger intensity of 8.7/10, battery performance is the #1 driver of customer churn and return rate growth.

💡

Design is the Bright Spot

Customers love how the product looks (Joy score: 8.2/10). Leveraging this in marketing while fixing underlying issues is a key strategic lever.

📈

Recovery is Achievable

Implementing the Top 3 Fixes — Battery, App Sync, Audio — is projected to recover customer sentiment by 34% within 12 months.
